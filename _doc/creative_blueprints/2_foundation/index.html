The Challenge & The Answer

Built to end
digital fragmentation.

The Problem

People juggle a dozen separate apps for daily life — different logins, different wallets, different notification streams. Every task is a context switch. The average smartphone user has 8–12 apps open daily, none of them talking to each other.

The Solution

Futo is one platform for your entire digital day. One identity. One wallet. One notification stream — for messaging, payments, shopping, and services. Built to feel like a single, coherent product, not a bundle of features bolted together.
